# Mela [መላ]
Your Stipend, Your Plan, Your Peace of Mind: Navigate University Life with Financial Clarity.

## Project Description
Mela is a financial scheduler app designed for Ethiopian university students to manage their monthly stipend, track expenses, and avoid financial stress. The app provides:
- Stipend & Essentials Setup
- Intelligent Dashboard & Projection
- Real-Time Expense Tracking
- Adaptive Spending Alerts
- Micro-Saving Goals
